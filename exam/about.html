Aarhus Sound System

     Aarhus Soundsystem is a tech startup in rapid development. In addition to our unique and sustainable concept for the production of speakers, we also offer event services, music production and booking. We are therefore involved in the entire audio process, from recording audio to playing it either at home or on a stage.

     Aarhus Soundsystem is built up as a community gathered around a common passion for audio. At our core, we consist of a team of 12 people who maintain the daily operations of the company, as well as the development of our commercial speakers. In addition to this, we have a lineup of business partners who are experienced in the services that are not directly related to speaker development and production. Speaker development and production Our goal is to innovate the production processes of speakers to eliminate or minimize the pollution from the materials used, as well as extend the life cycle of the speakers that we produce, which is in direct contrast to what the industry is currently characterised by. Every year, millions of speakers are discarded due to small faults, or outdated electronics. This is in no way sustainable, and is what we at Aarhus Sound System are aiming to eliminate - And you can be a part of that.

     Starting a new production is usually easier than altering existing production processes/income source. Therefore, we plan on producing products based on sustainable composite materials, recyclable materials, and a system of returning old models to ensure that proper recycling processes are followed. The speakers themself will offer all the functionality, sound quality and energy-saving technology that we expect from modern speakers.
