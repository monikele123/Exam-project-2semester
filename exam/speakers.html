The OSMAN speaker combines incredible sound quality with elegant and timeless design to produce a speaker that will satisfy even the most demanding acoustic and aesthetic requirements.
Our speaker uses a three-way setup to achieve a full, clean and accurate frequency response that satisfies even the most demanding audiophiles. A 1” dome tweeter from Scan-Speak delivers crisp notes in the higher frequencies, while a 4” inch midwoofer and an 8” woofer supplies a clean mid-range and a full low-range frequency response. Elegant Handcrafted Design

Speakers were designed to be elegant and subtle, combining classic design with modern strokes to complement and be a part of a rooms interior. The cabinets are made from solid oak and are handcrafted with love and care by our experienced woodworkers, thus ensuring unparalleled quality and a solid construction that is able to withstand the vibration-induced sound interference the speaker units produce.
