Setup for the big event

Aarhus Sound System runs a fleet of more than 10 Mobile Stages strategically located across Denmark. Our strong network, including our current Aarhus Sound System owners, offers a local service, a complete range of Mobile Stages and promotional units, and all of the necessary flexibility to answer quickly to any request; whether it is a single event or a national tour. By offering the highest level of service, a flawless safety track record and a constant availability of equipment, Aarhus Sound System remains the exclusive supplier of mobile stages to major event organizers throughout all Denmark. Aarhus Sound System brings innovation and efficiency to organizing exterior events.
