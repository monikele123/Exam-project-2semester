Spending all of your time and energy on speakers and equipment is not enough to get the perfect sound experience you need to have a perfect environment and since that's almost impossible to come across, sound diffuser is the next best thing.

Our acoustic sound diffusers are made from sustainable natural materials, with low to mid range being it's active region. It manipulates the way sound propagates through space, reducing the number of audible echos, thus giving the listener a feeling of a bigger listening space, where one can truly concentrate on the sound that's produced by the speakers.
