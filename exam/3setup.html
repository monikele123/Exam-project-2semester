3setup

The mobile rent out speaker is our first creation, and it reflects our values. It is made from upcycled materials, sound efficient drivers and has flashy lights.
It is capable of running for ~15 hours on a single charge, with center lights reacting to music and side lights spinning giving nice a cosy atmosphere for your event.
Made to withstand the elements with all the electronics hidden safely away. It's perfect for small outside events and arrangements.
